Hulun Ting tu, is the eighteenth map in "Heilongjiang quan sheng yutu," published in Xuangtong 3rd Year (1911).
(catalog record)

scale: 1,250,000:1 (as noted on map)
original plate size: 46cm X 36cm
scan resolution: 350dpi

This plate covers the area around present day Haila-er Shi and parts of Chenba'erhu Qi, Ewenkezu Zizhiqu, Yakeshi Shi, E'erguna Youqi, Xinba'erhu Zuoqi.

Geographic footprint:
Maximum North 50.95 N
Maximum South 47.20 N
Maximum East: 122.55 E
Maximum West: 117.70 E

Each map is printed on two pages, and each page has been scanned separately. The size of the uncompressed TIFF files is approximately 50 MB per page.
