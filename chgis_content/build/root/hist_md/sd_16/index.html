thumbnail of entire map:

 Tsing dschou fu, is sheet number G14, in the "Karte von Tschili und Schantung" [Maps of Zhili and Shandong] series published in 1907. Cartography by the Royal Prussian Land Survey. Printed in German romanization and Chinese Characters.

Scanned and compiled by Mark Henderson, Univ of California, Berkeley.

Funded by National Science Foundation award BCS-0201825.

scale: 1:200,000
