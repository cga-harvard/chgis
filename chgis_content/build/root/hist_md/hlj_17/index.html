Hulun Dao zongtu, is the seventeenth map in "Heilongjiang quan sheng yutu," published in Xuangtong 3rd Year (1911).
(catalog record)

scale: 1,750,000:1 (as noted on map)
original plate size: 46cm X 36cm
scan resolution: 350dpi

This plate covers the area around present day Haila-er Shi and parts of Manzhouli Shi, Xinba'erzhu Zuoqi, Chenba'erhu Qi, Ewenkezu Zizhiqu, Yakeshi Shi, E'erguna Zuoqi, E'erguna Youqi.

Geographic footprint:
Maximum North 53.50 N
Maximum South 47.15 N
Maximum East: 122.85 E
Maximum West: 116.45 E

Each map is printed on two pages, and each page has been scanned separately. The size of the uncompressed TIFF files is approximately 50 MB per page.
