Hejian Fu (Zhili), 1907

thumbnail of entire map:

 Ho dsien fu, is sheet number E12, in the "Karte von Tschili und Schantung" [Maps of Zhili and Shandong] series published in 1907. Cartography by the Royal Prussian Land Survey. Printed in German romanization and Chinese Characters.

Scanned and compiled by Mark Henderson, Univ of California, Berkeley.

Funded by National Science Foundation award BCS-0201825.

scale: 1:200,000
original plate size: 44cm X 37cm
scan resolution: 400dpi

This plate covers the area from Hejian Fu, heading east to Cang Zhou (Zhili) and south to the Nanpi Xian (Zhili).
