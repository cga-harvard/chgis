Hierarchical Regional Space (HRS): A Model of Spatial Differentiation
  • Reveals spatial processes operating at different scales across China's vast landscape 
  • Delineates a hierarchy of functional regions 
  • Differentiates a hierarchy of analytical zones 
  • Contextualizes all types of social phenomena 
  • Specifies the position of every locale within 
    • a macroregion's Core-Periphery Zones; 
    • an Urban-Rural Continuum; and 
    • an HRS zone that summarizes CPZ and URC
