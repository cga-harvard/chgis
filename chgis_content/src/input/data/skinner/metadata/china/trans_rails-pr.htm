B. Description

1. Abstract

Lines describing railroads shown on the Land Use Map of China and other sources from the 1990s. This information is published in a series of Arc/Info coverages, with one coverage for each of China's provinces, autonomous regions, and independent municipalities. Coverages are named Rails-pr, where pr is the two-digit identifer for each province.

2. Purpose

Spatial framework for transportation analysis at 1:1,000,000 scale.

3. Procedures Used (overview)

Digitized and assembled into provincial coverages by ACASIAN.

4. Sources and related Data Sets

Sources may include the Land Use Map of China and other published maps.

II. Data Quality Information

1. Intended area of coverage

Mainland China and Taiwan

2. Completeness

Complete.

3. Processing steps

These coverages will not be edited or verified by UCD. Railroads shown may include routes planned or constructed after 1990.
