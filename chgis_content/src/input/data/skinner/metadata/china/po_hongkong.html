B. Description

1. Abstract

Polygons define the boundaries of Hong Kong, its islands, territorial waters, District Board districts, and New Towns.

2. Purpose

Spatial framework for managing and analyzing tabular data compiled for Hong Kong districts in conjunction with an analysis of China at 1:1,000,000 scale.

3. Procedures Used (overview)

Boundaries were traced from a map published in the 1990 Hong Kong administrative yearbook (source map scale approximately 1:222,000). Traced boundaries were scanned, vectorized, and georeferenced to approximate the Hong Kong boundary shown in the China-A political boundaries coverage. Polygons were coded by name of their District Board and New Town names.

4. Sources and related Data Sets

Hong Kong Administrative Yearbook, 1990 courtesy of Center for Chinese Studies library, University of California, Berkeley.

County-level identification numbers from China-A Dataset (for 1990), University of California, Davis.
